Zehao Sha 沙泽浩

Currently, I am a third-year PhD student at Institut Fourier under the supervision of Gérard Besson and Hervé Gaussier.

From September 2021 to June 2022, I was a master's student at Institut Fourier under the supervision of Gérard Besson.

E-mail: shaz at univ-grenoble-alpes dot fr

My research area is differential geometry.

My PhD thesis focuses on Kähler-Ricci solitons and cscK metrics on bounded pseudoconvex domains.

Additionally, I am interested in problems related to scalar curvature, Kähler-Ricci flow, complex Monge-Ampère equations, and extremal Kähler metrics.
