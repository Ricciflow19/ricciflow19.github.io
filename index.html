Currently, I am a third-year PhD student at Institut Fourier under the supervision of Gérard Besson and Hervé Gaussier.

From September 2021 to June 2022, I was a master's student at Institut Fourier under the supervision of Gérard Besson.

E-mail: shaz@univgrenoble-alpes.fr or zhsha0251@gmail.com

I am interested in geometric analysis.

My PhD thesis focuses on canonical metrics on complete Kähler manifolds. Specifically, I study Kähler-Ricci solitons and cscK metrics on bounded pseudoconvex domains.

Additionally, I work on problems related to scalar curvature, Kähler-Ricci flow, complex Monge-Ampère equations, and extremal Kähler metrics.
